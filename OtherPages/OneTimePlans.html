One Time Coaching Plan Information

How It Works

For those who do not need the accountability or who are looking for a more cost-effective option than ongoing coaching, I will spend time to write a customized plan that you will follow on your own time.

When you sign up for the one-time plan, you will fill out an extensive questionnaire detailing your training and diet history, your goals, and your lifestyle. I will get on the phone with you to further understand your goals and ask further questions. After this information gathering process, I will deliver the plan for you (diet, training).
